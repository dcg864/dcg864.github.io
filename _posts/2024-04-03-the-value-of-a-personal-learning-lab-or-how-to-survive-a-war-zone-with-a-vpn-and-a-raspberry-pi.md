Join DC864 on April 4th for a presentation on the value of a personal learning lab, or How to Survive a War Zone with a VPN and a Raspberry Pi.

Every day learning is important. In my opinion, the best way to learn is to have a project and a playground. In this talk, I’ll present the technology stack in my personal lab, the evolution of how I’ve used it for professional development, and most importantly, the motivation behind the development and evolution. ## Speaker Bio

Mike Fletcher is a security leader with over 15 years in the security industry. He is an advocate of people leadership in the tech field, where most find only poor management. He also leads our Blue Team village.
