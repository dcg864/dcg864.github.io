Five years ago, we embarked on a journey with a vision to be an anchor point for local hackers, and today, it's amazing to look back at the innovation, collaboration, and friendships that have formed. It's our 5-year anniversary, and we're thrilled to invite you to a celebration!

**Event Details:**

- Date: **September 7**
- Time: **6:30 PM**
- Location: **Double Dogs** - 110 Milestone Way, Greenville, SC 29615
- Bills: Individual checks
- Give us the ol' thumbs up on the Discord announcement or RSVP in MeetUp if you plan on attending

**What to Expect:**

Regrettably, there isn't a dedicated group dining room available. However, we'll make arrangements to have as many tables positioned closely together as feasible. While there won't be a formal presentation, we're all brimming with captivating stories to share. This gathering presents an excellent chance to network and forge connections with fellow group members.
