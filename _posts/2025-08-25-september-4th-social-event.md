# Hangout at Local Cue

**Date:** Thursday, September 4th  
**Time:** 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM

  
**Location:** Local Cue, Greenville, SC  
**Type:** Hangout / Networking

Join DC864 at Local Cue for an evening of food, drinks, and good conversation. Whether you’re a regular member or this is your first time joining us, you’ll find a welcoming community of technology enthusiasts, hackers, builders, and problem solvers.

This month’s meetup is an open social hangout. There is no set agenda, so come ready to network, swap stories, and share ideas. If you have a project you’ve been working on, an experience you’d like to highlight, or a question you’ve been pondering, this is a great place to bring it up.

DC864 meetups are casual and inclusive. Expect to meet people with a wide range of technical interests, from cybersecurity and development to hardware tinkering and beyond. Whether you’re here to learn, teach, or simply connect with others in the community, you’ll fit right in.

Come hungry, come curious, and come ready to meet fellow members of the Upstate’s hacking and infosec community.
