Join us this November (11/03 at 6 PM) for the telling of tales from the field by community member rbrins. This will be an interactive experience where by you can take part in the action and get some representational experience on incident response in the corporate world.

We're also making a change to the project time. There will be three breakout areas:

- **Project Open Mic:** anyone can take five minutes to share what they're working on. This is a great opportunity to practice public speaking in a supportive environment.
- **Blue Team Village:** &drew will host Backdoors & Breaches
- **Red Team Village:** PNPT / OSCP study group

All meetings are free and open to the public.  
The meeting is both online and in-person.

- In-person at **Hughes Main Library** meeting **room A**.
