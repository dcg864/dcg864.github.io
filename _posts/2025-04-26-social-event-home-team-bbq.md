Join us May 1st, 2025 6pm - 7:30pm for a social gathering at Home Team BBQ.

Our meetings are great opportunities to get to know new people in the InfoSec community. Social events provide a lower key vibe, if that's even possible, from the regular monthly meetings. So if you enjoy InfoSec, technology, and good people come hang out with us.

Ask the host and some of us will be wearing DEFCON jolly hacker shirts.
