This Thursday 8/4 we have a special guest speaker from the **Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF), Alexis Hancock** Director of Engineering, Certbot.

Alexis works to encrypt the web by managing the Certbot and HTTPS Everywhere projects. She researches an intersection of issues on digital rights, encryption, and consumer technology. Deeply passionate about tech equity for all, she has been aiding activists and educators with their tech needs for over a decade.
