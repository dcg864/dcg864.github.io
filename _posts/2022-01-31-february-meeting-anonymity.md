This coming Thursday 2/3 we are going to start a series on online anonymity. Each following meeting will focus on a different aspect. No, this will not cover nefarious or illegal use cases. The February meeting will provide an overview of and different options available for online anonymity. For subsequent months there may be other speakers and topics as well with online anonymity being a brief portion of those meetings.

We'll see you 2/3 6-8 PM hybrid at Synergy Mill and online with Discord.

If you are interested in presenting a topic, and are local to the area, post a comment in the General Discord channel. A lead will reach out and guide you through the process.
