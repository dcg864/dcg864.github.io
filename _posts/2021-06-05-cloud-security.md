Most of our meetings are informal topics presented by a speaker. The June 2021 meeting was an open forum discussion of cloud security.

Several resources from the discussion:

- [Grayhat Warfare](https://grayhatwarfare.com/) searches for open AWS buckets and shorteners.
- [PACU](https://rhinosecuritylabs.com/aws/pacu-open-source-aws-exploitation-framework/) - AWS exploitation framework
- [BlobHunter ](https://github.com/cyberark/BlobHunter)is an opensource tool for scanning Azure blob storage accounts for publicly opened blobs.
- NIST special publication [800-207 Zero Trust Architecture](https://csrc.nist.gov/publications/detail/sp/800-207/final)
