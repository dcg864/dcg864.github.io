The June meeting will feature **you**. The main presentation time will meld with the project time as **Lighting Talks**. Please note the location change.

**Location:** **Pelham Road Library** | F.W. Symmes Branch | 1508 Pelham Rd · Greenville, SC

The rules of Lightning Talks are pretty simple:

- Everyone is encouraged to speak (*in line with our Code of Conduct*)
- No minimum time, 15-minute max.
- No recording.
- Share a project, get feedback on conference talk ideas, or something that interests you in security, privacy, digital rights, or technology (*broad scope*)

Fear not, we’re a friendly, supportive audience. This is the perfect environment to practice public speaking.
