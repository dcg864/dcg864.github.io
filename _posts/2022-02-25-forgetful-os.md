The March meeting will survey several operating systems that preserve your privacy and forget its use. We’ll demo [Whonix](https://www.whonix.org/), [Tails](https://tails.boum.org/index.en.html), and [Qubes OS](https://www.qubes-os.org/).

Thursday 3/3 is part 2 in our anonymity series. No, this will not cover nefarious or illegal use cases.

We'll see you 3/3 6-8 PM hybrid at Synergy Mill and online with Discord.

If you are interested in presenting a topic, and are local to the area, post a comment in the General Discord channel. A lead will reach out and guide you through the process.

We have a very open format, so join in and let us know what your interests are that you'd like to see from this group.

We leave an hour to going over member projects and interests. Only the main presentation is recorded and potentially posted to YouTube.
